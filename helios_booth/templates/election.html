To cast a vote, you will be led through the following steps.
If you have not yet logged in, you will be asked to do so at the very end of the process.

  1. Read these instructions carefully.

  2. Select your vote.
    You can easily navigate forwards and backwards.

  3. Review your choices.
    Your choices are encrypted safely inside your browser to keep your vote secret.

  4. Audit your choices (optional).
    To ensure that your vote is correctly encrypted, you can have the encryption verified. You can repeat this process as many times as you want, until you are convinced that this vote system functions correctly.

  5. Submit your encrypted choices.
    You will be asked to log in to submit your encrypted choices and you get a verification code.

Check your verification code.
At the end of the election, a list of verification codes that have been counted are published. If you want to confirm whether your vote has been correctly counted, you can look up your verification code in this list.
