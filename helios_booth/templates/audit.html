Verify your vote:

HOW?

  1. Select your vote verification info by clicking here
  2. copy this info to your clipboard (right-mouse click+Copy OR CTRL+C)
  3. use the Vote verifier to verify the info.
Once you're satisfied, click the "back to voting" button to re-encrypt your vote. You can then choose to cast your vote or verify it again. Before you go back to the voting system you can also post this vote to the a list of verified votes, this way anyone can verify this vote again.



Post vote [Optional]

Before going back to voting,
you can post this verified vote to the Helios tracking center so that others might double-check the verification of this vote.

Even if you post your verified vote, you must go back to voting and choose "Submit vote" if you want your vote to count.





IMPORTANT: this vote, now that it has been verified, can not be counted.
To submit a vote, you must click the "Back to Overview" button below, re-encryption is done automatically, and choose "Submit Vote" instead of "Verify Encryption".

Why re-encryption? By performing an verification your encryption as well as the randomness is made public, this means people can figure out who you voted for. Therefore a new encryption has to be made.
