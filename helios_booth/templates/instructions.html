Dear voter,
To cast a vote, you will be led through the following steps.
The first 4 steps (progress-image: the light gray blocks) are HTML-pages within your browser. This means there's no connection with the Helios-server and so no information can be passed through:

  1. Read these instructions carefully.

  2. Select your vote.
    You can easily navigate forwards and backwards.

  3. Check the overview of your choices.
    Your choices are encrypted (=coded) safely inside your browser to keep your vote secret.

  4. Verify the encryption (optional).
    To ensure that your vote is correctly encrypted, you can have the encryption (the encryption is a coded version of your vote) verified. You can repeat this process as many times as you want, until you are convinced that this encryption system functions correctly.
After the encryption and the optional verification you will need to log in to submit your vote (progress-image: dark blocks). A connection with the Helios-server will be made from here on but since you vote is encrypted it will remain a secret.
  1. Submit your encrypted choices.
    You will be asked to log in to submit your encrypted choices and you will also recieve a verification code. It is important that you save this code to later check whether your vote has been counted. When your voted is correctly submitted you will see a confirmation and you will also get a confirmation e-mail.

Check your verification code.
When your vote is submitted it is added to a list of votes that have been recieved. If you want to confirm whether your vote has been correctly counted, you can look up your verification code in this list.
